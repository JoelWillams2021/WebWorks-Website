Our Work Timeline

Step 1: Recieving A Quote

Duration 1-3 days

Recieving and reviewing the quote, which outlines the cost and scope of the project

Step 2: Meeting with CTO and CTF of WebWorks

Duration 1-2 weeks

Schedule a meeting with the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) and Chief Technical Officer (CTF) to discuss the project details.

Present your requirements and goals for the website.

Address any technical concerns or questions.

Receive feedback and suggestions from the CTO and CTF.

Agree on the terms and conditions of the project, including the payment schedule.

Step 3: Payment of half deposit for the product

Duration: Immediately after the meeting or within a few days, Make a payment of half the total cost as a deposit to initiate the development process.

Step 4: Getting a prototype and reviewing it

Duration: 1-2 weeks.

The development team creates a website prototype based on the agreed-upon requirements.

Review the prototype and provide feedback on its design, functionality, and usability.

Collaborate with the development team to make ay necessary revisions or improvements.

Step 5: Finish the website and the remaining payment

Duration: 1-4 weeks (depending on the complexity of the website)

The development team finalises the website based on the approved prototype and feedback.

Conduct thorough testing to ensure the website is fully functional and bug-free.

Once the website is completed to your satisfaction, make the remaining payment as per the agreed-upon terms.

Receive the final website files and launch the website.
